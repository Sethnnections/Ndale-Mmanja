About Ndale M'manja

Ndale M'manja is a revolutionary WhatsApp-based political education chatbot developed by the Malawian National Youth in Politics (MNYP). Our mission is to inform, engage, and empower young people by providing reliable, non-partisan political and civic education through interactive conversation.

We believe that an informed citizenry is the foundation of a strong democracy. By making political education accessible through WhatsApp, we're meeting young people where they are and making civic knowledge available to everyone.

Our chatbot uses simple language and interactive conversations to make complex political concepts easy to understand, helping build a more politically aware and engaged generation of Malawians.
